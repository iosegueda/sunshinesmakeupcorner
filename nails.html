Nails

Spring Sunset Ombre Nails


Ombré and gradient designs are super popular right now in the beauty world. You can find these two patterns in various aspects of the industry, like hair and makeup, but one of the most prominent places it can be found is on nails. Colorful, whimsical manis are one of the prettiest ways to sport these trendy designs and the technique used to create them is actually a lot easier to master than you may think. While the perfect blends you see in videos and photos may look too perfect to be easily recreated, the truth is, all you need is your favorite set of polish colors, some liquid latex, and a makeup sponge to do it

This technique can be used to create any gradient or ombré design your heart desires and works like a charm once you get the hang of it. And if you want to reuse the makeup sponge to create future looks, simply cut the top off with a pair of scissors, right under the layer of polish, to reveal a fresh, smooth surface.
